While living in Monroe, he learned how to crash dirt bikes and jump off of tall objects into the water. He currently lives in the quickly growing town of Bothell.
  • Former Career: Before embarking on his coding journey, Jason was a staff geologist who worked on hundreds of construction sites in Seattle and Los Angeles.
  • Favorite Hobby: Jason likes to spend his free time working in his vegetable garden and spending way too much money buying new games on Steam.
  • Favorite Food: Jason enjoys most foods that exist but especially enjoys quesadillas.
  • Future Goals: Jason plans on becoming a solid full stack Java Script Developer who will be able to save up enough money to one day finance and manage a food truck company called “The Quesadilla Factory”, which will cater toward fellow aficionados of quesadillas.
